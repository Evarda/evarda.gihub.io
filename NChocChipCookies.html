Nestle Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) Butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup Sugar
  • 3/4 cup packed Brown Sugar
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 2 large Eggs
  • 2 1/4 Cups Flour
  • 1 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 12 oz. semi-sweet Chocolate Chips (1 package)

Instructions

Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Combine butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract in bowl until creamy. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Mix in flour, baking soda, and salt. Stir in chocolate chips. Drop by rounded tablespoon onto ungreased baking sheets. Bake for 9 to 11 minutes or until golden brown. Cool on baking sheets for 2 minutes; remove to wire racks to cool completely.
