Dark Chocolate Chip Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 1 stick (1/2 cup) Butter
  • 2/3 cup Brown Sugar
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 4 Bananas (Very Ripe)
  • 1.5 cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1 cup Dark Chocolate Chips

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Butter and Flour a Bread Loaf Pan.
In a bowl, mix butter, sugar, egg.
In separate bowl, mash bananas until chunky/creamy - add to mixing bowl, mix.
In separate bowl, mix flour, baking soda, salt - add to mixing bowl, mix.
Add 3/4 chocolate chips, mix. Pour into Pan.
Sprinkle rest of chocolate chips on top.
Bake for 55-65 min.
