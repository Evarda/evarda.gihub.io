Meringue Cookies

Ingredients

  • 3 Egg Whites
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 4 drops Lemon Juice
  • 12 oz. Chocolate Chips

Instructions

Using mixer, beat egg whites until stiff peaks form. Gradually add in sugar. When well mixed, add lemon juice and mix in. Stir in 3/4 of bag of chocolate chips.
Use parchment paper on cookie sheets. Drop meringue by rounded spoonfuls onto paper. Bake cookies for 20 minutes at 250Â° then turn off oven and let set 30-40 minutes more. Makes about 40 cookies.
